Enhancementsedit

CCR
  • Reduce security permissions in CCR plugin #41391
Distributed
  • Avoid background sync on relocated primary #40800 (issue: #40731)
Infra/Packaging
  • Be lenient when parsing build flavor and type on the wire #40734 (issues: #39378, #40723)
