Kibana 7.11.0edit

For detailed information about the 7.11.0 release, review the Known issue, Deprecations, Enhancements, and Bug fixes.

For the 7.11.0 breaking changes, refer to breaking changes in 7.11.

Known issueedit

Dashboard

If you are using Kibana 7.9.x and earlier, and you upgrade to 7.10.0 to 7.17.2, all hidden panel titles now appear on your dashboards.

To resolve the known issue, upgrade to Kibana 7.17.3 or later.
