Delete role APIedit

[experimental] This functionality is experimental and may be changed or removed completely in a future release. Elastic will take a best effort approach to fix any issues, but experimental features are not subject to the support SLA of official GA features. Delete a Kibana role.

Prerequisiteedit

To use the delete role API, you must have the manage_security cluster privilege.

Requestedit

DELETE <kibana host>:<port>/api/security/role/my_admin_role

Response codesedit

204
Indicates a successful call.
404
Indicates an unsuccessful call.
