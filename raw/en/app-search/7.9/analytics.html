Analyticsedit

There are three different analytics endpoints that you can make requests against:

  • queries are search attempts performed against your engine.
  • clicks occur when someone clicks on the result of a query.
  • counts indicate the total number of queries and clicks within a time-frame.

Analytics log retentionedit

Since 7.9.0, App Search retains analytics according to your Enterprise Search log retention settings.

See Log retention in the Enterprise Search documentation.

Analytics log settingsedit

Since 7.9.0, you can disable analytics logging using the App Search log settings.

See Log settings guide.
