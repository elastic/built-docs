Functionbeat overviewedit

Functionbeat is an Elastic Beat that you deploy on your serverless environment to collect events generated by cloud services and ship the events to Elasticsearch.

The beta version supports deploying Functionbeat as an AWS Lambda service and responds to the triggers defined for the following event sources:

  • CloudWatch Logs
  • Amazon Simple Queue Service (SQS)

Functionbeat is based on the libbeat framework. For more information, see the Beats Platform Reference.
