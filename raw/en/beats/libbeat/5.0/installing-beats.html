Installing Beatsedit

After installing and configuring the Elastic stack, you need to install and configure your Beat.

Each Beat is a separately installable product. To get up and running quickly with a Beat, see the Getting Started information for your Beat:

  • Packetbeat
  • Metricbeat
  • Filebeat
  • Winlogbeat
