  • Do not emit error log when filestream reader reaches EOF and close.reader.on_eof is enabled. 31109

  • Fix type mapping of client.as.number in okta module. 31676

Metricbeat

Winlogbeat

  • Sysmon: Drop fields with "-" value (unset) 31556
