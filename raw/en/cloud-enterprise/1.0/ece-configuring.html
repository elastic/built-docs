Prepare Your Environmentedit

Get ready for Elastic Cloud Enterprise by preparing your environment for installation. To make getting ready easier for you, we have provided some tried-and-tested instructions for several environments:

  • Your Own Hosts:

    • Ubuntu 14.04 LTS (Trusty Tahr)
    • Ubuntu 16.04 LTS (Xenial Xerus)
    • Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) 7 and CentOS 7
  • Amazon Machine Images (AMI)
  • Google Cloud Platform (GCP)

Other cloud environments can also be used, provided that you prepare them to meet the prerequisites.
