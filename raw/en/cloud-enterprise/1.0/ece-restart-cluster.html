Restart a Clusteredit

To restart a running or a stopped cluster:

  1. Log into the Cloud UI.
  2. Click on a cluster name from the Clusters panel and click Manage.
  3. In the Danger Zone section, click Force restart or Restart cluster, depending on whether you had previously stopped the cluster. Let the user interface guide you through the steps for restarting a cluster.

If you had previously stopped the cluster, you can also edit the configuration of the cluster before restarting it.
