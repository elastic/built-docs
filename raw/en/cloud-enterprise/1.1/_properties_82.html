elasticsearch_clusters (array[MoveElasticsearchClusterConfiguration])
Optional list of Elasticsearch clusters to move off the allocator. If not specified, then all Elasticsearch clusters on the allocator are moved.

Optional list of Kibana clusters to move off the allocator. If not specified, then all Kibana clusters on the allocator are moved.
