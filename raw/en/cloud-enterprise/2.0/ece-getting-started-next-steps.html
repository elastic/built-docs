Next Stepsedit

Now that you have provisioned your first deployment and indexed some documents into your Elasticsearch cluster, try out some additional steps:

  • Enable additional features, such as Kibana, monitoring, and Graph.

    For Kibana 6.4.0 and later, you can use the one-click sample data, which allows you to use Kibana without loading your own data.

  • Configure Beats and Logstash to send data to Elastic Cloud Enterprise quickly with your Cloud ID.
  • Change your deployment configuration by upgrading to a newer Elasticsearch version or adding some plugins, for example.
  • Got existing Elasticsearch data? Consider your migration options.

Don’t forget to read our blog, which covers topics for both beginners and experienced users.
