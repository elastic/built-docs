Editing Your User Settingsedit

Elastic Cloud Enterprise provides two places in the console to customize Elasticsearch and Kibana to suit your needs. These editors append your changes to the appropriate YAML configuration file and they affect all users of that cluster. In each editor you can:

  • Dictate the behavior of Elasticsearch and the associated X-Pack settings.
  • Control your Kibana behavior, reporting, and other key settings.
