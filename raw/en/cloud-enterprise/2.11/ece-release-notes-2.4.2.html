Elastic Cloud Enterprise 2.4.2

edit

This version includes fixes for the following bugs and known issues:

  • Disappearing deployments with IP Filtering Ruleset associations.
  • Upgrading from 2.4.0 to 2.4.1 times out - please upgrade to 2.4.2 directly.
  • Installing or upgrading to 2.4.0 and 2.4.1 fails when IPv6 is disabled.

To upgrade to your Elastic Cloud Enterprise installation to this latest version, see Upgrade your installation.

Release date: November 14, 2019
