Restart a deploymentedit

To restart a running or a stopped deployment:

  1. Log into the Cloud UI.

  2. On the Deployments page, select your deployment.

    Narrow the list by name, ID, or choose from several other filters. To further define the list, use a combination of filters.

  3. In the Deployment Management section, click Restart and follow the steps to restart a deployment.

You can also edit the configuration of the deployment before restarting it.
