Preparing your installationedit

When you prepare your hosts to install ECE, the following prerequisites must be considered:

  • Hardware prerequisites
  • Software prerequisites
  • Networking prerequisites
  • High availability
  • Separation of roles
  • Load balancers
  • Users and permissions
  • JVM heap sizes
  • Wildcard DNS record
