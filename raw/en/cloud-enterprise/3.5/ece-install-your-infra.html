Install ECE on your own premisesedit

Before you start, make sure that your existing infrastructure meets the requirements.

ECE supports a wide range of OS versions. Here are some OS-specific instructions for preparing your hosts; other versions will be similar:

  • Ubuntu 20.04 LTS (Focal Fossa)
  • Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) 7 and CentOS 7
  • SUSE Linux Enterprise Server (SLES) 12 SP5
  • RHEL 8 using Podman

After your hosts are prepared, choose your preferred installation type:

  • Install ECE online
  • Install ECE offline
