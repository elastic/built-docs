Monitor your account activityedit

Information about your current Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku subscription charges is available directly from the Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku console and includes:

  • Current usage, per hour
  • Usage so far this month
  • Cluster activity, including the billable hours per cluster

To see your account activity:

  1. Log in to the Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku console.
  2. Go to the Account page.
