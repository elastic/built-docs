Delete your deploymentedit

To do that, select Delete deployment from the deployment overview page.

When you delete your deployment, billing stops immediately rounding up to the nearest hour.

To restore data from a deleted deployment, we recommend you to contact support as soon as possible within 30 days from the date of deletion. Billing restarts as soon as the deployment is restored.
