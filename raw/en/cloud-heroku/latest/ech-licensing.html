Subscription levelsedit

For more information on what is available with different subscription levels, check Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku Subscriptions. You are entitled to use all of the features in Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku that match your subscription level. Please use them to your heart’s content.

Your subscription determines which features are available. For example, machine learning and SAML authentication requires a Platinum or Private subscription and is not available if you upgrade to a Gold subscription.
