Support for APM Servers, automatic multi-factor authentication, and more features releasededit

Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku now supports additional features that let you do more with our hosted offering:

  • Multi-factor authentication enables automatically. When you add your first MFA device, instead of having to also enable MFA to set it up, now it gets enabled for you. Learn more …​
  • Tell us more about yourself. When you decide to take the plunge and add a credit card to your Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku account, we want to get to know you better and we’ll be asking for a bit more information. We use this information to design our products around your needs. Help us, help you.

Service release: January 24, 2019 (Milestone 15)
