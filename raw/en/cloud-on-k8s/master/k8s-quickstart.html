Quickstart

edit

With Elastic Cloud on Kubernetes (ECK) you can extend the basic Kubernetes orchestration capabilities to easily deploy, secure, upgrade your Elasticsearch cluster, and much more.

Eager to get started? This quickstart guide shows you how to:

  • Deploy ECK in your Kubernetes cluster
  • Deploy an Elasticsearch cluster
  • Deploy a Kibana instance
  • Update your deployment

Afterwards, you can find further sample resources in the project repository or by checking out our recipes.
