Delete your deployment

To do that, click Delete deployment from the deployment overview page.

Deleting cannot be undone and all data is lost. When you delete your deployment, billing stops immediately rounding up to the nearest hour. When ready, you can easily restart a deployment, run some tests, and tear it down again.
