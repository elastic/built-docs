GCP regions Asia East 1 (Taiwan), Europe North 1 (Finland), and Europe West 4 (Netherlands) availableedit

Elasticsearch Service is now available in these GCP regions:

  • Asia East 1 (Taiwan)
  • Europe North 1 (Finland)

  • Europe West 4 (Netherlands)

    You can select the new regions when you <<{p}-create-deployment,create new deployments>>.

Like other recently added regions, these support only HTTPS access for additional security. To learn more about what access different regions support, see Elasticsearch Service regions.```

Service release: May 5th, 2020
