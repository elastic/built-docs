Enhancements and bug fixesedit

The following changes are included in this release.

Enhancementsedit

Include relevant ICs in deployment info. The GET /api/v1/deployments/<id>?show_instance_configurations=true request will now return the list of instance_configurations used by the deployment in the response.

Widen discovery form to avoid line wraps. Widens discovery from from 475 to 600px to avoid text wrapping.
