Restart your deploymentedit

You might need to restart your deployment while addressing issues, like cycling garbage collection.

On the deployment overview, from the Manage drop-down menu select Restart.

You can choose to restart without downtime, by choosing which instances to restart, or you can restart them all simultaneously.
