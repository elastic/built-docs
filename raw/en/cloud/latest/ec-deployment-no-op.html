Run a no-op plan on your deploymentedit

When diagnosing or resolving issues with your deployment, it can be helpful to run a no-op plan (no operation plan) plan. This performs a rolling update on the components in your Elasticsearch Service deployment without actually applying any configuration changes. In some cases, warnings that appear on the Elasticsearch Service UI may be resolved simply by running a no-op plan.

To run a no-op plan:

  1. Log in to the Elasticsearch Service Console.

  2. Select a deployment.

    Narrow your deployments by name, ID, or choose from several other filters. To customize your view, use a combination of filters, or change the format from a grid to a list.

  3. From your deployment menu, go to the Edit page.
  4. Click Save.
