Subscription levelsedit

For more information on what is available with different subscription levels, check Elasticsearch Service Subscriptions. You are entitled to use all of the features in Elasticsearch Service that match your subscription level. Please use them to your heart’s content.

Your subscription determines which features are available. For example, machine learning and SAML authentication requires a Platinum or Private subscription and is not available if you upgrade to a Gold subscription.
