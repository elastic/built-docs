Manage your deploymentedit

Sometimes you might need to make changes to the entire deployment, a specific component, or just a single data tier.

  • Make adjustments to specific deployment components, such as an Integrations Server, APM & Fleet Server, Enterprise Search, Watcher, or Kibana.
  • Enable logging and monitoring of the deployment performance.
  • Disable a data tier.
  • Restart, stop routing, or delete your deployment.
  • Upgrade the Elastic Stack version for the deployment.
