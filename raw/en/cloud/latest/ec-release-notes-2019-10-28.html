GCP regions US East 4 (N. Virginia) and Europe West 2 (London) availableedit

Elasticsearch Service is now available in the GCP region US East 4 (N. Virginia) and Europe West 2 (London). You can select the new region when you create new deployments.

Like other recently added regions, N. Virginia and London support only HTTPS access for additional security. To learn more about what access different regions support, check Elasticsearch Service regions.

Service release: October 28, 2019
