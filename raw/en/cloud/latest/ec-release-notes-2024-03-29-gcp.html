GCP region Middle East West 1 (Tel Aviv) now availableedit

Elasticsearch Service is now available in the GCP ME West 1 (Tel Aviv) region. You can select the new regions when you create new deployments.

Like other recently added regions, Tel Aviv support only HTTPS access for additional security. To learn more about what access different regions support, check Elasticsearch Service regions.
