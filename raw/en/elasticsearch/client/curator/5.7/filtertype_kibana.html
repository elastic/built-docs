kibanaedit

- filtertype: kibana
  exclude: True

This filtertype will remove indices .kibana, .kibana-5, and .kibana-6 from the list of indices, if present.

This filtertype will iterate over the actionable list and match indices .kibana, .kibana-5, or .kibana-6. They will remain in, or be removed from the actionable list based on the value of exclude.

Optional settingsedit

  • exclude (default is True)
