Troubleshooting connectors

edit

Use the following actions to help diagnose and resolve issues with managed connectors and self-managed connectors:

  • View index health and ingestion status.
  • View errors, document count, sync history, and sync statuses.
  • View synced documents and index mappings.
  • Edit your index configuration: managed connector, self-managed connector.

You can also request help or support.
