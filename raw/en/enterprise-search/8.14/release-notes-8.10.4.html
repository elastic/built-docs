8.10.4 release notesedit

Upgrading to Enterprise Search 8.10.4? See Upgrading and migrating.

Enhancementsedit

  • Improved performance of Sharepoint Online incremental syncs by skipping Sites, Site Drives, Site Lists, Site List Items, and Site Pages that have a lastModifiedDateTime or Modified field older than the most recent sync’s start time.

Bug fixesedit

  • Fixed a bug where the Sharepoint Online connector did not add access control list entries for Azure Security Groups.
