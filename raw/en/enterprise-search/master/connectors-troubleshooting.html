Troubleshooting connectorsedit

Use the following actions to help diagnose and resolve issues with native connectors and connector clients:

  • View index health and ingestion status.
  • View errors, document count, sync history, and sync statuses.
  • View synced documents and index mappings.
  • Edit your index configuration: native connector, connector client.

For Workplace Search connectors, see the Workplace Search documentation.

You can also request help or support.
