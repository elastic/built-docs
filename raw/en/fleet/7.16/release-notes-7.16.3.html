Fleet and Elastic Agent 7.16.3edit

Review important information about the Fleet and Elastic Agent 7.16.3 releases.

Deprecationsedit

The following functionality is deprecated in 7.16.3, and will be removed in 8.0.0. Deprecated functionality does not have an immediate impact on your application, but we strongly recommend you make the necessary updates after you upgrade to 7.16.3.
