Running Kibana on Dockeredit

Docker images for Kibana are available from the Elastic Docker registry. The images are shipped with X-Pack installed.

X-Pack is pre-installed in this image. With X-Pack installed, Kibana expects to connect to an Elasticsearch cluster that is also runnning X-Pack.
