APMedit

Elastic Application Performance Monitoring (APM) automatically collects in-depth performance metrics and errors from inside your applications.

The APM page in Kibana is provided with the basic license. It enables developers to drill down into the performance data for their applications and quickly locate the performance bottlenecks.
