Kibana 6.8.16edit

The Kibana 6.8.16 release includes the following security update.

Details
In Kibana 6.8.15 and earlier, when a logged in user visits a maliciously created URL, Kibana could redirect users to an arbitrary website. CVE-2021-22141

Solution
Upgrade to Kibana 6.8.16.
