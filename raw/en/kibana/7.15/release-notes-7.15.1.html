Kibana 7.15.1edit

Review the following information about the 7.15.1 release.

Known issuesedit

Before you upgrade, review the known issues, then mitigate the impact to your application.

There are no known issues in 7.15.1.

For the known issues in the previous releases, refer to the <<known-issue-7.15.0, known issues in 7.15.0>.

Breaking changes can prevent your application from optimal operation and performance. Before you upgrade to 7.15.1, review the 7.15.0 breaking changes.
