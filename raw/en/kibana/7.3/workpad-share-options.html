Share your workpadedit

When you are ready to share your workpad, create a PDF, or export your workpad.

Create a PDFedit

To view your workpad outside of Kibana, generate a PDF.

  1. If you are using a Gold or Platinum license, enable reporting in your config/kibana.yml file.
  2. From your workpad, click the Share workpad icon in the upper left corner, then select PDF reports.

  3. Click Generate PDF.

Export your workpadedit

To share your workpad with another author, export it as a JSON file.

Export a single workpadedit

  1. From your workpad, click the Share workpad icon in the upper left corner.

  2. Select Download as JSON.

Export multiple workpadsedit

  1. Go to the Canvas workpads page.
  2. Select the workpads you want to export
  3. Click Export.
