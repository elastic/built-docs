Uptimeedit

The Uptime app in Kibana enables you to monitor the status of network endpoints via HTTP/S, TCP, and ICMP. You can explore endpoint status over time, drill down into specific monitors, and view a high-level snapshot of your environment at any point in time.

Get startededit

To get started with Elastic Uptime, refer to Install Uptime.
