Kibana Monitoring Metricsedit

To view the key metrics that indicate the overall health of Kibana itself, click Overview in the Kibana section of the Stack Monitoring page.

  1. To view Kibana instance metrics, click Instances.

    The Instances section shows the status of each Kibana instance.

  2. Click the name of an instance to view its instance statistics over time.
