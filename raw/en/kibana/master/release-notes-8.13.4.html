Kibana 8.13.4edit

The 8.13.4 release includes the following bug fixes.

Bug Fixesedit

Lens & Visualizations
  • Fixes table sorting when changing the interval on the time picker in Lens (#182173).
Dashboards
  • Fixes a bug with drilldowns where the control group on a source dashboard could be replaced by the control group from the destination dashboard (#179485).
