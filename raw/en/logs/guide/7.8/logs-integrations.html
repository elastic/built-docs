Integrate with Uptime and APM

edit

To see other actions related to the event, click Actions in the log event document details. Depending on the event and the features you have configured, you can:

  • Select View status in Uptime to view related uptime information in the Uptime app.
  • Select View in APM to view related APM traces in the APM app.
