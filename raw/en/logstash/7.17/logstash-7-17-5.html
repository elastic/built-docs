Logstash 7.17.5 Release Notesedit

Notable issues fixededit

  • Fix a possible corruption of Persistent Queue during a crash of the Logstash process #14147
  • Change pre-flight check for insufficient free disk space of a PQ to be a warning instead of an error #14267
  • Fix exception in logstash-keystore util when Logstash is configured with a deprecated setting #14246

Updates to dependenciesedit

  • The bundled JDK 11 has been updated to 11.0.15+10 #14152
