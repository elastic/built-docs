Metrics monitoringedit

If you haven’t already, you need to install and configure Metricbeat to populate the Metrics app with data. For more information, see Ingest metrics.

The Metrics app in Kibana enables you to visualize infrastructure metrics to help diagnose problematic spikes, identify high resource utilization, automatically discover and track pods, and unify your metrics with logs and APM data in Elasticsearch.

Using Metricbeat modules, you can ingest and analyze metrics from servers, Docker containers, Kubernetes orchestrations, explore and analyze Prometheus-style metrics or application telemetries, and many more.
