Manage data storage

edit

Universal Profiling provides the following ways to manage how your data is stored.

  • Index lifecycle management automatically manages your indices according to age or size metric thresholds. Universal Profiling ships with a default index lifecycle policy, but you can create a custom policy to meet your requirements.
  • Probabilistic profiling mode uses representative samples of profiling data to reduce storage needs even further.
