Unusual Discovery Signal Alert with Unusual Process Executableedit

This rule leverages Discovery building block rule alert data to alert on signals with unusual unique host.id, user.id and process.executable entries.

Rule type: new_terms

Rule indices:

  • .alerts-security.*

Severity: low

Risk score: 21

Runs every: 5m

Searches indices from: now-9m (Date Math format, see also Additional look-back time)

Maximum alerts per execution: 100

References: None

Tags:

  • Domain: Endpoint
  • OS: Windows
  • Use Case: Threat Detection
  • Tactic: Discovery
  • Rule Type: Higher-Order Rule

Version: 2
