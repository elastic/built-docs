AI Assistant use casesedit

Elastic AI Assistant’s flexibility means you can use it for many different purposes. These topics describe some of the possible uses for AI Assistant within Elastic Security:

  • Identify, investigate, and document threats
  • Triage alerts
  • Generate, customize, and learn about ES|QL queries

For general information about AI Assistant, refer to AI Assistant.
