Explore your data

edit

[preview] This functionality is in technical preview and may be changed or removed in a future release. Elastic will work to fix any issues, but features in technical preview are not subject to the support SLA of official GA features.

In addition to search, Elasticsearch Serverless offers several options for analyzing and visualizing your data.

  • Aggregations: Use the Elasticsearch REST API to summarize your data as metrics, statistics, or other analytics.
  • Discover: Use the Discover UI to filter your data or learn about its structure.
  • Visualizations: Build dynamic dashboards that visualize your data as charts, gauges, graphs, maps, and more.
  • Alerts: Create rules that trigger notifications based on your data.
