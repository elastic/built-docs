Elasticsearch

edit

[preview] This functionality is in technical preview and may be changed or removed in a future release. Elastic will work to fix any issues, but features in technical preview are not subject to the support SLA of official GA features.

Elasticsearch allows you to build custom applications. Whether you have structured or unstructured text, numerical data, or geospatial data, Elasticsearch can efficiently store and index it in a way that supports fast searches.

Understanding Elasticsearch Serverless

Refer to Serverless differences and Technical preview limitations for important details, including features and limitations specific to Elasticsearch Serverless.

Get started

edit
  • Create your Elasticsearch project: Create your first Elasticsearch project.

How to

edit
  • Search your data: Build your queries to perform and combine many types of searches.
  • Explore your data: Search, filter your data, and display your findings.
  • Manage alerts: Create rules to detect complex conditions and trigger alerts.
  • Use developer tools: Send requests with Console and profile queries with Search Profiler.
  • Manage your project: Manage user access, billing, and check performance metrics.
