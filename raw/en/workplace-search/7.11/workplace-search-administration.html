Administration and managementedit

Workplace Search provides a broad set of tools to help you and your team deploy, run and maintain a robust search experience at scale.

If you aren’t already up and running, read the Getting started guide for guidance through the initial setup steps.

Administrative tools are centered around a few core concepts:

  1. User management and security: Users are the atomic unit of Workplace Search and are easily managed and invited from the administrative dashboard.
  2. Group management: Users are organized within groups representing logical collections of functions and sources.
