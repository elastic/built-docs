Getting started with Workplace Searchedit

Elastic Workplace Search provides a fast, scalable, and relevant search experience for your everyday work life, across your entire organization. And it’s all Elastic under the hood. Workplace Search is packaged with App Search as a single solution named Elastic Enterprise Search.

Get started now with a free Elastic Cloud trial. Deploy Elastic Enterprise Search on Elastic Cloud or download and run the solution on-premises. Follow the steps in Installing Workplace Search.

After installing Enterprise Search, continue from the Workplace Search administration screen as follows:

  1. Connect content sources and unify searchable data
  2. Review user management and group management to bring the power of search to various teams in your organization
  3. Study the Searcher’s guide to teach your team mates The Ways of Search.
