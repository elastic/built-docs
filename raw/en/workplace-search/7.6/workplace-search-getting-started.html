Getting Started with Workplace Searchedit

Welcome to Workplace Search!

Workplace Search provides a fast, scalable, and relevant search experience for your everyday work life, across your entire organization. And it’s all Elastic under the hood.

This introduction assumes you have logged in and are looking at a fresh administration page. If you aren’t up and running yet, read the Installation guide for detailed instructions.

There are a few key steps needed to get rolling:

  1. Connecting Content sources and unifying searchable data
  2. Reviewing User management and security and Group management to bring the power of search to various teams in your organization
  3. Studying the The searcher’s guide to teach your team mates The Ways of Search.

Workplace Search beta feedback is always welcome and appreciated. Don’t hesitate to send us an email!
