Known issuesedit

The following known issues affect Workplace Search.

Also review each content source for known issues for that source.

  • Workplace Search connector permissions sync can’t be stopped

    When a Workplace Search connector is running and you try to disable its sync, the permissions sync continues to run.

  • Some thumbnail preview icons are not showing in the Workplace Search results list.

    This applies to Workplace Search versions 7.9.0 and later.
