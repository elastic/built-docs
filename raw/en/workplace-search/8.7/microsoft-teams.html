Teams connector packageedit

Use the Elastic Enterprise Search Microsoft Teams connector package to deploy and run a Microsoft Teams connector on your own infrastructure.

The connector extracts and syncs data from Microsoft Teams.

The data is indexed into a Workplace Search content source within your Elastic deployment.
